About Us

KMU has 17 schools, offering 4 disciplines in medicine, science, management and social science，17 Bachelor's Degree programs, 42 Master's Degree programs, and 1 Doctor's Degree program (including 17 fields). It has 7 research institutes, 11 affiliated hospitals, 9 teaching hospitals, 38practice hospitals, 9 forensic medicine sites, 6 preventive medicine practice sites, 4 pharmaceutical practice sites, and 2 optical practice sites.

  • In 1933, Donglu University initiated a specialized associate program in medicine and this has ever since evolved and grown into Kunming Medical University (KMU) of today.
  • The university campus occupies 109 hectares. It has over 15,000 students, of which more than 2,000 are postgraduate students.
  • The university has 6,750 staff and faculties, and over 1,450 are professors and associate professors.

Our Courses

MBBS

Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery

Students who aspire to practice medicine or become surgeons will have to take up this course before pursuing degrees concerning their preferred specialization.

BDS

Bachelor of Dental Sciences

Candidates who aspire to follow the career path of orthodontist or prosthodontist take up this course. BDS is a foundation course for those who want to become practising dentists or dental surgeons.

B.Pharm

Bachelor of Pharmacy

Candidates who want to work in the pharmaceutical sector or want to start their own pharmacies are required to pursue the B.Pharm course in order to understand different chemicals, medicinal salts and solutions used to cure people.

D.Pharma

Diploma of Pharmacy

Candidates who are looking for short-term courses in pharmacy can pursue this course as this is quite short than the B.Pharm programme. The course enables students to work in government pharmacies and assist in the medical divisions of hospitals.
